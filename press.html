About the Game

Short Description (80 characters)

Co-op puzzle game for 2–4 players. Solve together or fail together.

Medium Description (200 words)

Pippin Plaza is a mobile puzzle platformer with a 53-level story playable solo or in co-op parties of 2 to 4 — and in co-op, everyone escapes together or nobody gets out.

Create a party, share a 6-character code with your squad, and tackle 53 hand-crafted levels built so no one gets carried. Collect coins to unlock the key, grab the key to open the door, and get every player to the exit. Leave one person behind and you all fail.

Beyond the story mode, race the clock in Rush, take on the daily Survival quest gauntlet, or clear every level in one Marathon run. A teammate went down? They have 10 seconds to get back on their feet. Want to replay a brutal level? Every player must vote yes — unanimously. This is real co-op.

Long Description (full store listing)

There's no such thing as a solo carry in Pippin Plaza. Every player has to make it out — every single time.

HOW IT WORKS
Create a private party in seconds. Share the 6-character code with 1 to 3 friends. Jump in, coordinate your moves, collect coins, grab the key, open the door, and guide every Pippin character to the exit. Sounds simple. It isn't.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT
Most mobile "co-op" games let good players carry bad ones. Not here. Every puzzle in Pippin Plaza is built so all players must actively contribute. Stack on a teammate's shoulders to reach a high platform. Coordinate who grabs the key. Time your jumps together. Miss any one of these things and the whole team pays.

FEATURES
• 53 hand-crafted co-op puzzle levels
• Rush, Survival & Marathon modes — timed runs, daily quests, full-game marathons
• 2 to 4 players per online party
• 6-character party code — share with anyone, anywhere
• No account required
• Revive system — a downed player has 10 seconds to get back up
• Replay voting — unanimous yes required
• Global leaderboards
• Free to play (ad-supported)
