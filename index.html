How It Works

Three steps. Zero excuses.

Getting into a game with your friends takes about 30 seconds. Escaping together might take a little longer.

01
🏠

Create a Party

Tap "Create Party" and get a 6-character code. Share it however you want — text, voice, vibes.

02
🧩

Solve Puzzles Together

Collect coins, grab the key, open the door. Each level is designed so nobody can finish alone.

03
🚪

Everyone Exits

Every player must reach the exit. Leave someone behind and nobody wins. Real co-op.

Features

Built different.

🧩

53 Puzzle Levels

Hand-crafted stages that escalate in complexity. Every level needs actual coordination — not just button mashing.

👥

2–4 Players

Play with 2, 3, or 4 — every configuration works. More players means more coordination required, more chaos guaranteed.

🌐

Play Online Anywhere

No local Wi-Fi required. Powered by real-time networking — play with friends across the globe. No excuses.

⏱️

Rush, Survival & Marathon

Beyond the story: race timed Rush challenges, take on the daily Survival quest gauntlet, or clear every level in one Marathon run.

⚡

6-Character Party Codes

No account required. Share a code, friends join instantly. Games start in seconds, not minutes.

🔄

Revive System

Fell into a spike? You've got 10 seconds to get back on your feet before the team pays the price. Move fast.

🏆

Leaderboards

Compete for the top spots on global leaderboards. Speed, precision, teamwork — every run counts.

🎮

Free to Play

No paywalls, no subscriptions. Download and start playing immediately. Your whole squad can join for free.
