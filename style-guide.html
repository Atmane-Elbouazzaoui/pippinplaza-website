12. Gaps

Gaps & opportunities

Current-state notes
  • Interactive visibility and expansion rely on state classes such as .open and JS on the live site; the CSS alone does not provide a no-script behavior pattern.
  • Several layout and color tweaks are applied inline in the HTML pages rather than through reusable utility or component classes.
